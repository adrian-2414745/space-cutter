Objective: cut as much as possible, as fast as possible, without intersecting the balls during cutting. Controls: Arrow keys - move the cutter around the perimeter Space - to begin cutting in straight line; hit Space again to turn the cutting to closest edge P - Pause

How to Play

Goal: Slice the rectangle down to under 15% of its original size before the timer runs out.

Move scissors: Swipe left/right for counter/clockwise movement. Double tap to cut.

Cut: Double-tap the canvas to initiate a cut from the scissors' current position.

Avoid balls: If a ball touches the cut line, the cut fails and scissors reset.

Scoring: Each successful cut scores points. Smaller slices = more points.
